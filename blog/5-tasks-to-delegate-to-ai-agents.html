5 Tasks You Should Delegate to AI Agents Today

Start here. Thank me later.

1

Content Repurposing

Taking one piece of content and transforming it into multiple formats.

Example: Video → Blog post → Twitter thread → LinkedIn post → Newsletter

Perfect for agents because it's formulaic transformation with clear input/output.

⏱️ Saves 4-6 hours per piece
2

Competitor Research

Systematic analysis of what competitors are doing.

Example: Pricing comparison, feature matrix, content audit, social media analysis.

Agents don't get bored with repetitive research. Structured output works well.

⏱️ Saves 8-10 hours per analysis
3

Email First Drafts

Initial drafts of emails you send repeatedly.

Example: Sales outreach, customer follow-ups, partnership inquiries.

Email has clear structure. Similar emails are templateable. You always review before sending.

⏱️ Saves 1-2 hours per batch
4

Meeting Prep

Research and briefing documents before important meetings.

Example: Company background, attendee summaries, recent news, talking points.

High value, low effort. Directly impacts meeting quality.

⏱️ Saves 30-60 min per meeting
5

Process Documentation

Writing down how you do things so others can replicate.

Example: Onboarding checklists, how-to guides, SOPs, FAQs.

Documentation is tedious. Once done, useful forever.

⏱️ Saves 2-4 hours per document

The Common Thread

These tasks share key traits:

  • Clear inputs and outputs — You know what you're giving and getting
  • Repeatable — You do them regularly
  • Verifiable — You can check if output is correct
  • Low-risk — Mistakes are catchable
  • High time-cost — They take hours when done manually

This is the sweet spot for AI delegation.
